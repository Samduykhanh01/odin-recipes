Pizza

Description:

Pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Garlic Oil
  • Pizza Toppings

Steps:

  1. Make the dough
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill
  4. Flip the crust and brush with oil
  5. Top the pizza however you like, cose the lid, and cook until the cheese melts
